The best AI video prompts are specific enough to guide the model, but short enough to stay focused. A prompt for a short clip should usually describe one shot, one subject, one main action, and one visual mood.

If you ask for a full commercial, a multi-scene story, or a detailed sequence of edits, the result may feel compressed or inconsistent. If you describe one strong shot, you give the model a clearer target.

## A simple prompt formula

Use this structure:

```text
Subject + action + setting + camera movement + lighting + style
```

You do not need every part in every prompt, but this formula helps you avoid vague instructions.

Basic example:

```text
A red fox walking through a snowy forest, soft snowfall, low tracking shot, overcast natural light, realistic cinematic style.
```

Why it works:

- the subject is clear: a red fox
- the action is clear: walking
- the setting is clear: snowy forest
- the camera is clear: low tracking shot
- the mood is clear: soft, realistic, cinematic

## Product shot prompts

Product prompts work best when you describe the object, surface, light, and movement. Keep the scene clean. One product on one surface is often stronger than a cluttered setup.

```text
A perfume bottle on black stone, mist drifting around it, slow push-in camera, dramatic rim lighting, luxury product commercial style.
```

```text
A ceramic coffee cup on a wooden desk, morning light through a window, slow push-in camera, calm lifestyle product shot.
```

```text
A pair of running shoes on wet asphalt, water droplets on the surface, low angle tracking shot, cool blue lighting, energetic sports commercial style.
```

```text
A wireless headphone case opening on a clean white table, soft studio light, slow macro shot, minimal technology product style.
```

How to adapt these prompts:

- Replace the product with your object.
- Replace the surface with a setting that fits the brand.
- Keep camera movement simple: push-in, pan, orbit, tracking shot, or locked-off shot.
- Add lighting details only if they matter to the mood.

## Social media clip prompts

For social content, the best prompt is often a visual hook. It should create motion quickly and be easy to understand without sound.

```text
A cozy desk setup at night, laptop screen glowing, coffee steam rising, slow push-in camera, warm ambient light, calm creator workspace.
```

```text
A creator packing a small camera bag on a clean table, overhead shot, smooth fast motion, natural daylight, practical lifestyle style.
```

```text
Colorful sticky notes spreading across a whiteboard, quick overhead movement, bright office light, energetic productivity style.
```

```text
A phone on a desk receiving a calendar reminder, close-up shot, soft morning light, clean minimal workspace, realistic style.
```

## Cinematic scene prompts

Cinematic prompts benefit from mood, camera distance, and light. Instead of writing “make it cinematic,” describe what makes the shot cinematic.

```text
A lone cyclist crossing a foggy bridge at sunrise, wide shot, soft blue light, slow tracking camera, cinematic realism.
```

```text
Rain falling on a neon street at night, reflections on wet asphalt, slow dolly movement, moody film look.
```

```text
A small boat drifting across a quiet lake at dawn, wide static shot, pale orange sky, gentle mist, poetic cinematic style.
```

```text
A person standing at the edge of a desert road, wind moving their coat, wide shot, golden hour light, slow cinematic zoom.
```

If the result becomes too dramatic or chaotic, reduce the number of mood words. “Foggy bridge at sunrise” is more useful than “epic emotional breathtaking cinematic masterpiece.”

## Website and landing page prompts

Website clips usually need to be clean, loop-friendly, and not too distracting. Avoid busy subjects if the clip will sit behind text.

```text
Abstract blue light waves moving across a dark glass surface, slow smooth motion, minimal futuristic style, clean background for a landing page.
```

```text
Soft paper cards sliding into a neat stack on a white desk, top-down shot, gentle shadows, clean productivity style.
```

```text
Tiny glowing particles moving through a dark workspace, slow motion, subtle blue light, minimal technology background.
```

```text
A blank notebook opening on a desk, pen resting beside it, slow overhead shot, soft natural light, calm editorial style.
```

For landing pages, review the result with text placed over it. A beautiful clip can still fail if it makes headlines hard to read.

## Prompt patterns to avoid

Avoid prompts that ask for too much at once:

```text
Create a 30-second ad with a product intro, three lifestyle scenes, customer reactions, text overlays, and a final logo.
```

Avoid prompts that depend on exact readable text:

```text
Show a sign that says “Launch Sale 50% Off” in perfect typography.
```

Avoid vague prompts:

```text
Make a viral AI video about productivity.
```

Better:

```text
A clean desk transforming from messy to organized, overhead shot, smooth motion, bright morning light, productivity visual metaphor.
```
